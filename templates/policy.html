Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is very important. Waqtu discloses its privacy policy so you know what information it collects, why it collects them, and what we do with them. This service is maintained and operated solely by muslim community all over the world. Waqtu does not collect your location or other similar information unless you choose to provide it. It does collect other limited information automatically from visitors who read, browse, and/or download information from the web app. It does this so we can understand how the web app is being used and how it can make it more helpful. The bottom line is that the service keep users information confidential and does not sell any information that it get after your sign up process to any third party. Waqtu values user's privacy and collect their information only after their consent so that users can make the most of the features and have a great overall experience.

1. Information it collects

The information it collect from you comprises of the general information that it collect from all users and some specific information that you provide upon registering on Waqtu . The information it gather is only for improving your experience and giving you better versions of our features.

1.1 When you browse:

Certain information about your visit can be collected when you browse our web app. When you browse Waqtu, it, and in some cases our third-party service providers, can collect the following types of information about your visit, including:

  • IP address (an IP or internet protocol address is a number that is automatically assigned to a device connected to the web)
  • Approximate geographic location based on the IP address of the user’s local system or internet service provider.
  • Operating system (which is software that directs a computer’s basic functions such as executing programs and managing storage) for the device that you are using and information about the browser you used when visiting the web app.
  • Date and time of your visit.
  • Pages you visited.
  • Address of the website that connected you to Waqtu (such as google.com)
  • Device type (desktop computer, tablet, or type of mobile device)
  • Screen resolution
  • Browser language
  • Geographic location
  • Time spent on pages
  • Scroll depth – The measure of how much of a web page was viewed
  • User events (e.g. clicking a button)
  • Cookies of prayer times setting i.e. Calculation method, juristic method, Latitude and Longitude of your selected location.
It use this information to measure the number of visitors to our web app and its various sections, to help make our web app more useful to users by adding more features.

1.2 General information and permissions:

The general information we collect is applicable to all our Waqtu users. All these permissions except for the user’s device information is obtained only after the user’s consent. Whenever we collect any information of the user, it is transmitted to us in a secure way. The following permissions are taken on app level that we require from you:

  • Location information is required for providing our users accurate Prayer Times.
  • Notification permission is required for sending prayer times and other helpful reminder notifications.
Users can always change permissions settings from their respective browsers.

2. How we use your information

The information that you provide is completely safe with us as our users’ safety is our utmost priority. We have taken special safety and security measures to ensure data protection of our valued users. The information that you provide us is purely and solely used to give you a personalized experience and to improve any shortcomings our app has in order to facilitate you. We use your data to provide, support, personalize and develop our Services.

2.1 Cookies:

Waqtu uses cookies to improve the users experience while browsing/visiting the web app. This web app uses a cookie control system which enables the user to allow or disallow the use of cookies on their computer / device upon their first web app visit. This complies with recent legislation requirements for web apps to obtain explicit consent from their users before leaving behind or reading files such as cookies on a user's computer / device.

2.1.1 Policy:

Cookies are small files that are saved to the user's computer's hard drive which track, save and store information about the user's interactions and usage of the web app. This allows the web app to provide its users with a personalized experience. In order for our users to disallow or discontinue the use and saving of cookies from this web app on their computer's hard drive, necessary steps will have to be taken within their web browsers security settings which will block all web app cookies and it's external serving vendors. This web app uses built-in tracking software that comes with respective installed browser to monitor it's visitors to better understand their web app usage.

2.1.2 Cookies we use:

When you use or access our Services, we may place several cookies files in your web browser. We use cookies for the following purposes; to enable certain functions of our Service, to provide analytics and to store preferences. Our site does not keep or use any personal information. Any anonymous information is handled according to our Privacy Policy as seen on our web app. We only use cookies to get accurate Prayer Times according to users location. We are using following cookies:

  • Calculation Method (Prayer times calculation based on this cookie)
  • Current detected Location (To calculate exact prayer times according to users location)
  • Last detected location (Save users previous city location so that he can change location easily from recent location option)
However, you may prefer to disable cookies on this site and on others. The most effective way to do this is to disable cookies in your browser. We suggest consulting the ‘Help’ section of your browser, but be aware that certain functionality may not be available to you.

Waqtu may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. We may provide you additional forms of notice of modifications or updates as appropriate under the circumstances. Your continued use of Waqtu service and its products after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification. Please note that Waqtu would be able to access only what the user wants to share. Any information shared with us by the user is completely protected and secure. Keeping our user's personal information safe is our utmost priority.
